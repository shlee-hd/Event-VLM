# Release and Risk Team

## Mission
- Prevent last-mile regressions and ensure submission-grade integrity.

## Responsibilities
- enforce release checklist before each version snapshot.
- verify consistency between paper numbers and artifact outputs.
- track unresolved high-severity risks.
- approve/hold final promotion of claims into abstract and conclusion.

## Gate Checklist
- all headline numbers linked to reproducible artifacts.
- reviewer P0/P1 issues resolved or explicitly deferred.
- no unresolved equation correctness concerns.
- no formatting blockers (major overflow, broken references, missing assets).
- versioned snapshot created and changelog updated.
