# Writing and Figure Team

## Mission
- Make the paper persuasive, elegant, and easy to parse under reviewer time pressure.

## Responsibilities
- refine abstract/introduction/conclusion for precision and beauty.
- maintain one-claim-one-evidence mapping in prose.
- improve figure storytelling and caption quality.
- enforce layout polish:
  - no overflow
  - balanced whitespace
  - readable table density

## Figure/Table Standards
- title communicates purpose in one line.
- caption explains what to read first and why it matters.
- axis labels/units complete and unambiguous.
- color/shape choices remain legible after grayscale printing.

## Writing Standards
- avoid vague adjectives without numbers.
- avoid overclaiming when confidence intervals are missing.
- keep terminology stable across sections.
